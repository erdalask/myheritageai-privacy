MyHeritage AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

MyHeritage AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear: MyHeritage AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • Photos: All photos you upload are processed entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to your photos.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location.
  • Analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services.

How the App Works

MyHeritage AI analyzes facial features using on-device processing to provide entertainment heritage estimates. All analysis happens locally on your iPhone. No data leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases for analysis credits. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Entertainment Purpose

MyHeritage AI is designed for entertainment purposes only. The heritage estimates provided are AI-generated based on facial features and do not represent actual genetic ancestry or DNA analysis.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as rated 4+ on the App Store.
